# PRODUCT.md — oksasatya.dev

## What this is

Personal portfolio of **Oksa Satya** — full-stack developer (backend-focused) who builds
business operations systems: HRIS & payroll, POS, inventory, and multi-tenant SaaS.
The site is itself a proof of craft: it must look and perform like the work it sells.

## Register

**Brand** — design IS the product. Marketing surface, portfolio genre.
Aesthetic lane: technical-editorial, light, spacious. Anchors: Linear/Stripe-grade
restraint applied to a business-systems engineer's identity — NOT a copy of their look.

## Audiences (two, one story)

1. **Indonesian freelance clients** (primary, Bahasa Indonesia) — SME owners / managers
   searching "jasa pembuatan website/aplikasi/API/toko online". They need to trust that
   he can build systems like the ones shown.
2. **Remote employers/recruiters** (secondary) — assess seniority through case-study
   depth, architecture decisions, and code links. English layer ships post-launch
   (`/en` minimal: home, about, Dexova, contact — phase 2).

## Conversion actions

- Primary: WhatsApp conversation ("Diskusikan Proyek") — no backend form.
- Secondary: read Dexova case study → contact; GitHub/LinkedIn for recruiters.

## Flagship proof

**Dexova** — integrated ERP SaaS for Indonesian businesses (dexova.id):
HRIS/Payroll (PP 35/2021 tiered overtime, PPh 21, BPJS), Attendance PWA (geofence,
dual-address WFH), POS (shift + cash reconciliation, Midtrans QRIS), Inventory
(multi-warehouse), Tax services. Three app surfaces: `dex-fe` (admin), `dex-attendance`
(employee PWA), `dex-pos` (cashier).

## Hard rules

- No fabricated metrics, testimonials, or claims. Verifiable proof only.
- Compliance topics (PPh 21, BPJS, PP 35/2021) framed as "business rules I implemented",
  never tax/legal advisory authority.
- All product screenshots pass a redaction check (no tenant/employee/financial data,
  no API keys, no staging URLs) before publication.
- Existing URLs are preserved (SEO equity). No renames without 301s.
