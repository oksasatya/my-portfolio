# Product Marketing Context

*Last updated: 2026-07-10*

## Product Overview
**One-liner:** Oksa Satya builds business operations systems — HRIS & payroll, POS, inventory, and multi-tenant SaaS — from database to UI.
**What it does:** Personal portfolio that converts two audiences: Indonesian SMEs buying custom web/app development, and remote employers hiring a senior full-stack (backend-focused) developer. Flagship proof is Dexova, his integrated ERP SaaS.
**Product category:** jasa pembuatan website/aplikasi (ID market) + developer portfolio (hiring market).
**Product type:** Personal brand site / services.
**Business model:** Freelance projects (WhatsApp-first funnel) + employment opportunities.

## Target Audience
**Target companies:** Indonesian SMEs/UMKM needing operational systems (HR, kasir, stok, web apps); international companies hiring remote full-stack engineers.
**Decision-makers:** business owners, ops managers (clients); engineering managers, recruiters (hiring).
**Primary use case:** "I need someone who can build a reliable business system end-to-end."
**Jobs to be done:**
- Build/replace an operational system (HRIS, POS, inventory, dashboard, API integration).
- Assess whether this developer is senior enough to trust with production systems.

## Problems & Pain Points
**Core problem:** generic agencies/devs deliver brochure sites; business systems need domain logic (payroll rules, stock reconciliation, payments) done correctly.
**Why alternatives fall short:** template portfolios show logos, not systems; claims without proof.
**Emotional tension:** fear of paying for software that breaks in production or never ships.

## Differentiation
**Key differentiators:**
- Built and operates a real ERP SaaS (Dexova) with regulation-aware payroll (PP 35/2021 tiered overtime, PPh 21, BPJS), geofence attendance PWA, POS with shift/cash reconciliation + Midtrans QRIS, multi-warehouse inventory.
- 4+ years building enterprise applications for a Dubai software house (Java/Spring Boot + Next.js).
- Backend depth (Go, Java, Laravel, Node) AND ships polished frontends (Next.js/React).
**Why customers choose:** proof-of-work over promises — live products, deep case studies, code.

## Objections
| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "Can a solo dev handle our system?" | Dexova: three production app surfaces built and operated solo/small-team; case study shows scope control. |
| "Is he really senior?" | Case studies expose architecture decisions, failure modes handled, compliance logic — recruiters can verify on GitHub. |
| "How much does it cost?" | Free WhatsApp consultation; scope-based estimate; process transparency on /jasa pages. |

## Customer Language
**Words to use:** sistem, aplikasi bisnis, HRIS, payroll, kasir/POS, stok/inventory, dashboard admin, integrasi (payment/HRIS/logistik), production, multi-tenant SaaS.
**Words to avoid:** "digital experiences", "passionate", "cutting-edge", "world-class", unverifiable superlatives, tax/legal advisory claims.

## Brand Voice
**Tone:** professional, direct, technically grounded; Bahasa Indonesia primary.
**Style:** outcome-based storytelling (problem → decision → result), active voice, specifics over adjectives.
**Personality:** precise, load-bearing, calm, credible.

## Proof Points
**Verifiable:** 4+ years enterprise work (PT Infini, Dubai, since July 2022); Dexova live at dexova.id with documented HRIS/POS guides; three app surfaces (dex-fe, dex-attendance PWA, dex-pos); Midtrans QRIS integration; Hacktiv8 Golang certification (2024); B.Sc. Information Systems (Gunadarma).
**Do NOT use:** dexova.id marketing claims (client counts inconsistent on-site), Google reviews (they belong to the tax-consulting service), template testimonial photos (deleted).

## Goals
**Business goal:** freelance inquiries (WhatsApp) + remote job conversations.
**Conversion action:** WhatsApp chat start; secondary: case-study read-through, GitHub/LinkedIn visit.
